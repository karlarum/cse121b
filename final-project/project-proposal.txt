Project Title: League of Legends Champion Information
 
What will be built?
It will show League of Legends champion information including pictures

What will it do? 

It will filter through the champions with the following filters:
1. Controller
2. Fighter
3. Mage
4. Marksman
5. Slayer
6. Tank
7. Specialist
8. Difficulty (it will sort difficulty from lowest to highest)
9. All (It will show all champions)

What will be the source of your external data?
https://ddragon.leagueoflegends.com/cdn/12.6.1/data/en_US/champion.json
